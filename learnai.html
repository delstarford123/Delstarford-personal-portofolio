Overview

LEARN AI is a virtual learning platform designed to connect aspiring AI students with expert instructors and curated resources. It offers structured courses, interactive labs, and mentorship opportunities to help learners build real-world AI skills from anywhere.

Key Features

  • Structured AI curriculum with beginner to advanced tracks
  • Interactive coding labs and real-world projects
  • Resource library with tutorials, datasets, and research papers
  • Live mentorship and discussion forums
  • Progress tracking and certification upon completion

Technology Stack

Frameworks & Libraries: Flask, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Pandas, NumPy

Deployment: Vercel

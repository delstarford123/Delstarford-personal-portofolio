InsectAI

AI-powered system that listens to insect sounds and identifies species in real-time using deep learning and audio analysis.

What is InsectAI?

InsectAI is an intelligent system that identifies insect species from their sounds using a neural network trained on spectrograms. It combines real-time audio capture, signal preprocessing, and machine learning to assist researchers, farmers, and environmentalists in monitoring insect populations efficiently.

Insect AI creates a bridge between senses: it provides the insect image when offered the audio, and generates the insect's sound when offered an image.

How It Works

The AI model listens to the surrounding environment through a microphone or audio file, extracts key sound frequencies, and predicts the insect type. Why It Matters

Insects are vital to ecosystems and agriculture. Detecting them early helps in pest control, biodiversity tracking, and disease vector monitoring. Farmers lose billions annually to pests that could have been detected by sound before they were visible to the eye.

InsectAI provides an affordable and scalable way to analyze insect activity without the need for expensive field equipment, ensuring food security and environmental balance.

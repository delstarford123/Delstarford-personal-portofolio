What is InsectAI?

InsectAI is an intelligent system that identifies insect species from their sounds using a neural network trained on spectrograms. It combines real-time audio capture, signal preprocessing, and machine learning to assist researchers, farmers, and environmentalists in monitoring insect populations efficiently.

How It Works

The AI model listens to the surrounding environment through a microphone or audio file, extracts key sound frequencies, and predicts the insect type.

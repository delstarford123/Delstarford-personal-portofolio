FREEZE AI — Intelligent Refrigerator Maintenance

AI-powered system for early detection of spoilage and predictive alerts for food freshness and equipment health.

Overview

FREEZE AI is designed to revolutionize cold storage management by using artificial intelligence to monitor temperature patterns, detect anomalies, and predict spoilage timelines. It helps reduce food waste, optimize energy usage, and ensure safety in refrigeration systems across homes, restaurants, and supply chains.

Key Features

  • Real-time temperature and humidity tracking
  • Predictive spoilage detection using AI models
  • Voice and visual alerts for maintenance and freshness
  • Analytics dashboard for inventory and shelf-life insights
  • Mobile notifications and remote monitoring

Technology Stack

Frameworks & Libraries: Flask, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Pandas, NumPy

Deployment: Vercel

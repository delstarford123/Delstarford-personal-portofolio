FREEZE AI — Intelligent Refrigerator Maintenance

AI-powered system for early detection of spoilage and predictive alerts for food freshness and equipment health.

Overview

FREEZE AI is designed to revolutionize cold storage management by using artificial intelligence to monitor temperature patterns, detect anomalies, and predict spoilage timelines. [Image of smart refrigerator IoT sensor architecture diagram]

It helps reduce food waste, optimize energy usage, and ensure safety in refrigeration systems across homes, restaurants, and supply chains.

Key Features

  • Real-time temperature and humidity tracking via IoT sensors
  • Predictive spoilage detection using AI models trained on fruit decay imagery
  • Voice and visual alerts for maintenance (e.g., "Door left open" or "Filter change needed")
  • Analytics dashboard for inventory and shelf-life insights
  • Mobile notifications and remote monitoring

Technology Stack

Frameworks & Libraries: Flask, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Pandas, NumPy

Hardware Integration: Raspberry Pi / ESP32 for sensor data ingestion

Deployment: Vercel

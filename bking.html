BKING — AI-Intelligent Banking for Farmers

Empowering small-scale farmers and business owners with smart financial services, insurance access, and emergency credit solutions.

Overview

BKING is an AI-powered banking assistant designed to bridge the gap between rural entrepreneurs and financial institutions. It uses intelligent profiling and predictive analytics to connect users with insurance providers, offer early emergency loans, and reduce financial risks in agriculture and small businesses.

Core Features

  • Risk profiling for insurance matching
  • Emergency credit and loan recommendations
  • Mobile-first interface for rural accessibility
  • Financial behavior analysis and forecasting
  • Agricultural risk alerts and market insights

Technology Stack

Frameworks & Libraries: Flask, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Pandas, NumPy

Deployment: Vercel

AI-powered surveillance for wildlife parks and reserves, enabling early detection of poaching, environmental threats, and abnormal animal behavior.

Overview

KWS AI is designed to enhance conservation efforts through intelligent monitoring of wildlife habitats. By integrating sensors, cameras, and AI models, the system provides real-time alerts and predictive insights to park rangers and conservationists, helping protect endangered species and maintain ecological balance.

Key Features

  • Real-time animal movement tracking and anomaly detection
  • Environmental condition monitoring (temperature, humidity, pollution)
  • Alerts for poaching activity and unauthorized access
  • Predictive analytics for migration and health trends
  • Voice-assisted navigation for field operatives

Technology Stack

Frameworks & Libraries: Flask, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Pandas, NumPy

Hardware: IoT sensors, GPS modules, surveillance cameras

Deployment: Vercel
